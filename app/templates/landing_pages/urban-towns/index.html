FRESH URBAN LIVING


Take a trip to fresh urban living in Whitby. We are excited to announce a new community coming to the picturesque corner of Promenade Drive and Garden Street in Whitby, Fresh Urban Towns. This new development will feature a combination of stacked & traditional townhomes that back onto protected wetlands; it’s where modern meets nature.

FOOTSTEPS AWAY FROM SHOPPING & DINING


Convenience is a stone’s throw away as Taunton Garden Plaza is within walking distance making shopping and dining always accessible.

NATURE IN YOUR BACKYARD


Bike, walk or hike along any one of the surrounding trails with access to 125 parks, 950 acres of active greenspace and over 60 km of trails to explore. With numerous recreational choices in this community, they offer you acres of green space for exploration.

NEIGHBOURHOOD AMENITIES

Beautiful Nature Trails

Play in its multitude of lush parks and playgrounds, or bike, walk and hike along any one of the surrounding trails. Lovers of water sports and activities are right at home along Whitby’s waterfront that offers an opportunity for canoeing, kayaking, or simply strolling down the boardwalk. Its sandy beaches are also great for soaking up the sun or family picnics.

Neighbourhood Parks and Schools

Education is on the forefront with several public and separate schools in the area, in addition to post-secondary institutions such as Durham College’s Whitby campus and the University of Ontario, Institute of Technology.

Convenient Access to Transit

Commuting is made easy with access to Highways 401 and 407. GO Transit is at your doorstep, with both train and bus access to downtown Toronto in less than 1 hour.
