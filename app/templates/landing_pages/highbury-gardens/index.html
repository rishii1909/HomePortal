About Highbury Gardens in Whitby





Highbury Gardens are a collection of single detached family homes and townhomes in Whitby within walking distance of the city’s downtown, as well as offering easy access to the rest of the GTA.

The new homes and townhomes of Highbury Gardens in Whitby are within walking distance of the city’s downtown, as well as offering easy access to the rest of the GTA




Current plans indicate that Highbury Gardens will consist of 96 townhomes and detached single-family homes, with the unit mix being as follows:


  • 9 detached single-family homes (all freehold), with sizes between 2,194 and 2,262 square feet.

  • 72 street townhomes (14 of which will be freehold), with sizes between 1,638 and 2,145 square feet.

  • 15 laneway townhomes, with sizes between 1,830 and 1,983 square feet. 

Highbury Gardens in Whitby Location


Highbury Gardens will be located near the intersection of Garden Street and Dundas Street East near Downtown Whitby with easy access to Highway 401.



WHITBY COMMUNITY


Highbury Gardens lies in the heart of Whitby, and will be within walking distance of all that the city’s historic downtown has to offer:

  • The shops and restaurants of Downtown Whitby are just steps away.

  • The famous school from which this development takes its name is a short distance away.

  • No Frills, FreshCo, Sobeys, Metro, Foodland and a host of independent grocers are all located nearby.

  • Whitby Entertainment Centrum is a short drive to the south.

  • Whitby Harbor, Ronald Deeth Park and a number of other lakefront recreational spaces are just a short drive or bus ride to the south.

Durham Region Transit provides frequent bus service along Highway 2 via their Pulse bus rapid transit route. For access to Downtown Toronto, Whitby GO station is readily accessible by car or public transit. From there, trains on the Lakeshore East rail line run every 30 minutes or better seven days a week. Drivers can take advantage of easy access to Highway 401.
