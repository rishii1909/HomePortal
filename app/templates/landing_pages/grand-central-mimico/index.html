Mimico is one of the most historically significant neighbourhoods in Toronto.


Established over 150 years ago.


Offers residents a vibrant lifestyle.


Multiple parks & ample retail.

Mimico Triangle

 

Toronto’s Greatest Redevelopment Story

Accessibility


The new Kipling Transit Hub is set to revolutionize transit in the area. Intricate connectivity and a variety of transit options will enable residents to venture out and explore every corner of the GTA.

  • In 2013 Toronto's city council adopted an official plan amendment to completely regenerate this neighborhood.
  • The “Regeneration Area (RA)” designation is very rare, with Mimico being only one of seven areas in the city redesignated as “RA” 
  • The intention of the RA designation:​
    • ​accommodate significant employment
    • create massive residential growth
    • build new density around major transit infrastructure (Mimico Go Station)
    • provide enhanced green spaces for future residential growth (new 12 acre park)
    • The newly received RA designation resulted in a massive land acquisition spree by some of Ontario’s largest developers
    • It took 8 years to plan this community from amendment adoption to the launch of the first project, the Buckingham
    • This is one of the best investment opportunities in the entire GTA to get in on the ground floor of a massive transformation!
