Location


Seaton Village is one of Toronto’s most exclusive residential neighborhoods. It is home to some of the city’s most desirable and historic homes, while also being close to several vibrant commercial and retail districts – most notably the Annex:


  • Nearby Bloor Street is home to an eclectic mix of shops and restaurants, and is known as one of the city’s best areas for dining out.
  • Koreatown is directly to the south, and is a vibrant and thriving urban district
  • Both Sobeys and Loblaws have locations within walking distance, while a Metro and numerous independent grocers are also nearby.
  • ​The University of Toronto is two subway stations to the south, making this a convenient location for students and professors alike.
  • ​The historic and picturesque Casa Loma is just to the northeast.

​

Dupont Station on TTC subway Line 1 is a short walk to the east, and provides direct access to Downtown Toronto as well as destinations to the north (such as Yorkdale Shopping Centre). Bathurst Station on Line 2 is also nearby, and is accessible via a short walk or bus ride. In addition, frequent bus service is available on both Bathurst and Dupont Streets.
