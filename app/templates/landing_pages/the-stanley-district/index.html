EXCITING NEW NIAGARA DEVELOPMENTS

Today Niagara is the very definition of Bright Lights Big City – with its shimmering casinos, world class entertainment, hundreds of restaurants, uninterrupted night life and attractions that bringing over 30 million people a year.

The city is all grown up now, with a new super hospital on the drafting table, a new 5,000 seat entertainment centre under construction, and expanded GO Trainservice linking Niagara to the Hamilton-Toronto economy.

The New Niagara Falls is energetic, smart and rife with opportunities! From its parks, recreation areas, and the 130 wineries and 30 golf courses that dot its magnificently landscaped regions, Niagara is bursting with growth and progress.
